The Banned Books Series

If you are here, you saw a painting and you were curious about it. That's great! Curiosity - the need to find and know the truth - is a force for change.

Each of these paintings represents a book that has been banned. Most were banned by the United States Naval Academy and are now targeted in schools, libraries, and other institutions across the U.S. These books display some of the rich diversity of race, culture, self-expression, and beliefs being silenced by threats to intellectual freedom and democracy itself.

Some of them tell the stories of communities to whom the U.S. has failed to keep its promise, over and over again, while they built our nation into what it is. Some of them tell of those who hid in the shadows to survive, then came into the light with the promise of equality, only to be threatened with un-personhood again. Some are mere entertainment, but contain plotlines and characters that give authentic glimpses into the human mind and heart.

Please, keep reading. Keep questioning. Keep thinking. Be the change.
