The Banned Books Series

Each of these paintings represents a book that has been banned. Most were banned by the United States Naval Academy and are now targeted in schools, libraries, and other institutions across the U.S. These books contain some of the rich diversity of race, culture, self-expression, and beliefs that are endangered by attacks on intellectual freedom and democracy itself.

Some of them have been banned in an effort to deny the existence, personhood, or value of many people who built our country or make it what it is today. Some present uncomfortable history or inconvenient truths. Some are mere entertainment, but their authors' self-expression gives authentic insights into lived experience that bans attempt to un-write.

To resist being un-written, all we have to do is read. So, please, share these paintings. Keep reading. Keep questioning. Keep thinking. We can be the change.

Please share the title of a book that has been banned so it can be represented here.
